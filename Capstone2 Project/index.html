Kirk Self Care

Kirk Self Care is an e-commerce business that provides consumers a fun and easy way to take part in self care in style with elegance and convenience.

Business Plan

What problem do we solve?

Lots of people don't have enough time to take care of themselves and most don't want to invest the time for important self care, even though they may know its worth, self care can be an undesired skill. Well, our products present a counter and provide an engaging, enjoyable, convenient and easy way to be able to practice and perform self care wherever you are at. Our competitive advantage is not only do we focus on holistic self care and well being, but we have products acclimatable to all environments in your life such as work, bath, living room, and car.

Target market

Busy people who stay on the go and people who need to be taken care of but don't have anyone for needs of yourself (college students, CEO's, Divorced). Grinders, busy people, people that could care less

Barriers to entry

  • Acquiring customer loyalty
  • Capital requirements
  • Effective communication to target market
Above are barriers of entry to this industry and market. We will overcome these barriers by:
  • Producing strong marketing campaigns for brand recognition
  • Being innovative and offering new solutions
  • Getting reviews to build a good reputation
